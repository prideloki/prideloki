My 3 favourite productivity apps
Jul 18, 2015
3 minutes read

There are certain apps that people use everyday or couldn’t live without them. Some of them are social media apps. But I would like to share my productive apps.

Pocket These app helps me get rid of bookmarks for read later. Whenever i find an interesting article, i usually use the pocket chrome extension. the chrome extension is easy to use, just click the icon and it will save the article. I can read articles on any devices like PC(Website, Mac), Mobile(iPad,iPhone). On my iPhone, i can share an article to pocket as well. I love that this app is on any devices and it syncs nicely with other devices. The saved articles on my pocket app keep growing so I tried to read some of them while I am commuting. I would recommend my friends who keep lots of tabs on their browser to use Pocket. It saves you times.

Evernote this is the most popular note-taking platform. I love their Web clipper chrome extension. I can’t live without it. It makes saving content on website more easier. I keep those content for later references. I also keep my journals and goals on Evernote. Tips for those who can’t seem to stick with writing Journal, I suggest you to use the IFTT service to create your note everyday at a certain time. That will cut the cost of creating note everyday so you can focus on start writing Journal instead. I’m a premium Evernote user. I have faced issues a couple time with my syncing notes. I had contacted a customer support service. They helped me as much as they could to solve my issues since I provided them detailed steps I took to produce issue and solve issues( they don’t have the right to access my note content so I have to fix it myself with their helpful instructions).

Asana Asana is a project management application. But you can use for your personal management. I’ve been using Asana for almost a year. What I love about Asana is the My Tasks view, there is a list divided by 4 sections: New Tasks, Today, Upcoming, Later. This allows me to focus on Today tasks and see what tasks are waiting for me in the Upcoming section. I can promote my tasks from Upcoming to Today if I want to and put them back if there are more important and urgent tasks come up.

This is my very first blog, Plus my English is my second language. Hope whom ever come across this blog find this useful though ;P

Have a nice day.
